About Mahama Refugee Camp

Mahama Refugee Camp in Rwanda is home to thousands of refugees who have fled conflict and hardship. These families face daily challenges in accessing basic necessities like food, clean water, healthcare, and education. Our mission is to bridge the gap between those who want to help and those who need support.
